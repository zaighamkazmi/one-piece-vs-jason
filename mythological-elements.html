Mythological Elements

One Piece

"One Piece" is rich with supernatural elements, most notably the Devil Fruits, which bestow extraordinary powers upon those who consume them. The series also features an array of mythical creatures, such as the gigantic Sea Kings and various legendary figures that add depth and wonder to its expansive world.

Jason and the Argonauts

In "Jason and the Argonauts," divine intervention plays a crucial role, with gods like Hera and Athena guiding and manipulating events. The myth is populated with formidable creatures, including the harpies that torment Phineus and the dragon that fiercely guards the Golden Fleece, each encounter testing the mettle of Jason and his companions.
